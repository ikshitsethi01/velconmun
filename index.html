About Velcon Model United Nations

Velcon Model United Nations 2026 hereby invites applications from interested students and participants to participate in the upcoming edition of the conference scheduled on the 16th and 17th of May 2026. Velcon MUN 2026 aims to provide a dynamic and intellectually rigorous platform that encourages debate diplomacy leadership and a deep understanding of global affairs while upholding the highest standards of professionalism and academic excellence.

The venue for Velcon Model United Nations 2026 shall be formally notified in due course through official communication channels. All applicants and prospective participants are advised to regularly check for updates and announcements released by the organising committee.

Conference Details

  • Dates: 16th and 17th May 2026
  • Location: Faridabad, NCR
  • Format: Offline Conference
  • Email: info@velconmun.in

From high intensity crisis simulations to policy driven multilateral forums, Velcon MUN 2026 is structured to challenge delegates intellectually and diplomatically. This edition redefines debate through innovation, precision and institutional realism.
