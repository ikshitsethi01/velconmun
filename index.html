About Velcon Model United Nations

Velcon Model United Nations is the flagship deliberative platform of The Veritas Group, designed to explore the intersection of diplomacy, governance and emerging global challenges. Built on rigorous debate and futuristic agendas, Velcon MUN offers delegates a space to engage with policy making beyond conventional frameworks.

The conference emphasizes analytical depth, crisis management and real world applicability, ensuring that every participant experiences diplomacy as it unfolds in contemporary international systems.

Conference Details

  • Dates: 10th and 11th April 2026
  • Location: Delhi NCR
  • Format: Offline Conference
  • Email: munverizon@gmail.com

From high intensity crisis simulations to policy driven multilateral forums, Velcon MUN 2026 is structured to challenge delegates intellectually and diplomatically. This edition redefines debate through innovation, precision and institutional realism.
